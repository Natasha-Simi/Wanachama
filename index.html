About Wanachama

This is a web application that provides small investment groups with a safe and convinient platform to communicate and manage all matters pertinent to the group's workings.

  • What is Wanachama?
  • Why use Wanachama?

What is Wanachama?

Wanachama is a web application that provides a platform for small investment groups to organize all their information. It allows the members to upload minutes of meetings they have had, generate bank statements as well as other financial statements and even allow communication between members.

The easy and efficient retrieval of information should suffice decision making amongst the members.

Why use Wanachama?

Transparency. The generation of group reports by all members of the investment group, viewing of loans and fines accrued as well as approval of most decisions by group members elevate the level of transparency.

Organization. The Wanachama interface allows the members to organize their reports and minutes in a way that makes it easy to quickly access needed information.

Efficiency. The application is easy to use and is available 24/7 on any device with access to a web browser.
